About Me

Welcome to my page, my name is Madalina Chiveri! I am a full-time student at Miami University. I am a Games + Simulation major with a focus in game art. Under my major, I am an ET Leader. I come from Brunswick, Ohio which is 20 minutes south of Cleveland. My hobbies include drawing, writing, composing, and learning languages. My goals after my studies at Miami are to become a full-time concept artist and game developer, and create my very own game that I entirely develop, illustrate, translate, and compose the music for. This video game has been under production since summer 2021.
